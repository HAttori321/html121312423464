TAG
noun
  1. 1. a label attached to someone or something for the purpose of identification or to give other information.
  2. "he gave his pet a collar with a metal name tag"
  3. synonyms: label, ticket, badge, mark, marker, tab, tally, sticker, docket, stub, chit, chitt, counterfoi, flag, stamp
  4. "a price tag"
  • 2. a small piece or part that is attached to a main body.
    • VERB
    1. attach a label to.
    1. "mothers suspected that their babies had been wrongly tagged during an alarm at the hospital"
    2. synonyms: label, attach tags to, put a label on, mark, ticket, earmark, identify, docket, flag, indicate
    3. "the bottles were tagged with colour-coded labels"
  • 2. add to something, especially as an afterthought or with no real connection.
    1. "she meant to tag her question on at the end of her remarks"
    2. synonyms: add, tack, join, attach, append, affix, annex
    3. "their heads were so large that the rest of their bodies seemed tagged on as an afterthought"
